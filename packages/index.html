About Quartermaster

Quartermaster is an integrated Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) toolchain that implements industry best practises of license compliance management.

Quartermaster runs adjacent to a software build in CI or development environments. It collects build graphs, performs analysis and generates compliance reports (for developers, reviewers, upstream and downstream).

Quartermaster focuses on fact finding and accurate, complete and up-to-date compliance documentation.

Quartermaster is developed under an openly governed collaborative model. For more information, visit the Quartermaster website, or follow the @fosscompliance Twitter account.
